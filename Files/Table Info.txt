====================================================
NEO4J RELATIONSHIPS
====================================================

(Customer)-[:USES]->(Device)

Cardinality:
M:M

Reason:
- One customer can use multiple devices
- One device can be used by multiple customers


(Customer)-[:MADE]->(Transaction)

Cardinality:
1:M

Reason:
- One customer can create many transactions
- One transaction belongs to one customer


(Customer)-[:MATCHES]->(SanctionEntity)

Cardinality:
M:M

Reason:
- A customer may match multiple watchlists
- A sanction entity may match multiple customers


(Customer)-[:HAS_BEHAVIOR]->(Behavior)

Cardinality:
1:M

Reason:
- Behavior records are time-series snapshots


(Transaction)-[:TO]->(Beneficiary)

Cardinality:
M:1

Reason:
- Many transactions can go to one beneficiary


(Transaction)-[:AT]->(Merchant)

Cardinality:
M:1

Reason:
- Many transactions can occur at one merchant


(Beneficiary)-[:LINKED_TO]->(FraudCase)

Cardinality:
M:M

Reason:
- One beneficiary may appear in multiple fraud cases
- One fraud case may involve multiple beneficiaries
